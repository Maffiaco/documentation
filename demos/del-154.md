  - ## Issue
- The existing **bill module** had a rigid structure.

- Adding or updating document types (e.g., invoices, food checks) required changes in multiple places.

### [TP] [DELIANY-3242](https://deliany.youtrack.cloud/agiles/131-3/current?issue=DELIANY-3242) [Bug][Backoffice] Wrong quantity additions in sales reports
 - ## Issue

- In the **backoffice sales reports**, quantities of **product order additions** were not aggregated correctly.
- Additions belonging to the same `addition_id` and `price` could appear multiple times instead of being merged.
- This caused the reported quantities of additions in sales reports to be inaccurate.

 - ## Solution

- Implemented the `merge_product_order_additions/1` function to properly aggregate product order additions.
- Grouped additions by `addition_id` and `price`.
- Calculated the total quantity using `Decimal.add/2` to ensure precision.
- This ensures that quantities of additions are correctly merged and accurately displayed in the sales reports.
