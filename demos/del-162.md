### [TP] 🚀 [DELIANY-3319](https://deliany.youtrack.cloud/agiles/131-3/current?issue=DELIANY-3319) [POS] Add overpayment warning for payment amount exceeding 10% of remaining payable amount

PR: https://github.com/Maffiaco/sicily/pull/3502

**Issue:** On POS, staff can enter or select a received amount much higher than the remaining payable amount without any confirmation, which risks incorrect overpayments.

**Solution:**
- Show a confirm modal when the received amount exceeds **10%** above the rounded amount due (`Math.ceil`)
- Apply on both suggested amount selection and payment form submit
- **Continue** keeps the selected amount and sends a Sentry warning (Slack alert pipeline)
- **Cancel** discards the overpayment action
- No warning when amount ≤ threshold or equals pay-full

### [TP] 🚀 [DELIANY-3320](https://deliany.youtrack.cloud/agiles/131-3/current?issue=DELIANY-3320) [BackOffice] Add tab "Payments" & show payment log details for bookings

PR: https://github.com/Maffiaco/sicily/pull/3503

**Issue:** Booking details in Back Office had no dedicated Payments view, so users could not review payment status, history, deposits, or transaction details without leaving the flyout.

**Solution:**
- Add a **Payments** tab in booking details (Dine-in / Banquet)
- Show payment summary: total, paid, remaining, and status (Unpaid / Partially Paid / Paid)
- Show payment log table sorted by latest payment date/time
- Include deposit, full, payment-link, integrated, manual, cancelled, failed, and refunded records
- Calculate paid amount from successful payments only; deduct completed refunds
- Empty state: "No payment records found."
