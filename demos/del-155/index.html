[Nam] DELIANY-3252 Allow add sale channel price(s) with Schedule Update Price

Previously, a scheduled price change applied a single price uniformly. This change extends price schedules to support per-sales-channel pricing — so a product can be scheduled to have different prices on different sales channels at a future point in time.

  • Price schedules now accept a list of channel-specific prices. When creating a schedule, user can provide a price per sales channel instead of (or in addition to) a single global price.
  • A "Keep price" toggle allows users to leave the global or per-channel price unchanged rather than requiring a new value
  • When a scheduled price job runs, it merges the new channel prices with the product's existing prices, updating only the channels specified in the schedule and leaving others untouched.
