### [Nam] [DELIANY-3220](https://deliany.youtrack.cloud/issue/DELIANY-3220) Allow user retry NAPAS payment creation after timeout/deposit on confirm order flow

Allow retrying payment after it has expired or been cancelled.
Previously, only expired payments could be retried; now cancelled payments are also supported.
Deposit payments are filtered out from the current order payment lookup,
and QR code status is polled on an interval to detect expiration in real time.

- Added `Cancelled` to able-to-retry payment statuses, and filtered out deposit payments.
- Replaced `useEffect` with `useInterval` (1s polling) to continuously check payment expiration/cancellation.
